# fastcashflow

A fast IFRS 17 cash flow projection engine. Model points and an actuarial
basis go in; the insurance contract liability comes out -- the best estimate
liability (BEL), the risk adjustment (RA) and the contractual service margin
(CSM), under the general measurement model, the premium allocation approach
and the variable fee approach.
